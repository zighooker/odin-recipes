Mac 'n Cheese

For when you have 10 minutes and can't be bothered

Ingredients:

  • 1 box of prepackaged mac and cheese
  • 2 tsp butter
  • 2 tsp sriracha

Instructions:

  1. Bring 1 qt of water to boil
  2. Lower heat to medium, add pasta
  3. Cook for 8 minutes stirring on occasion
  4. Melt butter in microwave
  5. Mix with cheese powder and sriracha
  6. Add cooked pasta
  7. "Enjoy"
